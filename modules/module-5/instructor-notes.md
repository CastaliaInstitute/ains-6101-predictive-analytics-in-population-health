# Module 5 Instructor Notes

## Teaching Goal

Students should use **equity and social determinants** to make a defensible technical or managerial decision.

## Common Pitfalls

- Treating model output as self-validating
- Skipping baseline comparisons or stakeholder constraints
- Reporting metrics without explaining operational meaning
- Omitting privacy, safety, governance, or deployment limits

## Facilitation

Start with a concrete failure case, then ask students what evidence would have prevented it. Keep critique focused on assumptions, evidence, and system boundaries.

## Grading Cue

Reward clear reasoning about tradeoffs and limitations. Do not reward unnecessary complexity when a simpler baseline answers the question.
